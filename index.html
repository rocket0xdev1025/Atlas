Meet ATLAS – The Official Dog of the USA

Atlas is a proud German Shepherd and the beloved companion of Vice President JD Vance. As the First Dog of the United States (DOTUS), he’s become a familiar face across the nation. During the Trump–Vance campaign, Atlas quickly captured the hearts of supporters with his loyal presence, calm confidence, and unmistakable charm.

Who is Atlas?

Atlas isn’t just a presidential pet; he’s a symbol of loyalty, strength, and the down to earth spirit many Americans admire. Known for his sharp intelligence and gentle temperament, Atlas often accompanies the Vice President on trips, events, and behind the scenes moments at the White House.

Whether he’s greeting visitors, exploring the grounds, or simply enjoying a well earned nap, Atlas has become a beloved figure in his own right. Fans across the country follow his adventures, celebrating him as a true four-legged ambassador of the nation.

How To Buy

Create a Solana Wallet

Download a Solana-compatible wallet like Phantom or Solflare from the App Store, Google Play, or as a browser extension. Set up your wallet and securely store your recovery phrase.

Get Some SOL

Make sure you have SOL in your wallet to swap for $ATLAS. You can buy SOL directly in your wallet, transfer it from another wallet, or purchase it on an exchange (like Binance, Coinbase, etc.) and send it to your wallet.

Go to a Solana DEX

Open a Solana decentralized exchange such as Jupiter, Raydium, or Orca in your browser or inside your wallet app. Connect your wallet, then paste the official $ATLAS token address and select it.
